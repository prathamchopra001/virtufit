How it will work.

1

Body Scan

Customer stands in front of the smart mirror. Depth sensors and cameras capture full body measurements, create a 3D mesh, and analyze complexion. Facial recognition creates or retrieves their account automatically. Total time: 3 seconds.

2

Garment Selection

Customer browses the available catalog through touch interface or voice. They select an item they want to try. Our AI recommends their ideal size based on body measurements and historical data.

3

Virtual Try-On

The system renders the selected garment on their body in real-time. Physics simulation ensures realistic movement and draping. If the garment exposes skin not visible in their current clothing, our AI generates matching skin texture seamlessly.

4

Size Comparison

Customer can instantly switch between sizes (S, M, L, XL) or view multiple sizes side-by-side. They can rotate, zoom, and examine from every angle. The system highlights fit differences to help them decide.

5

Decision & Purchase

Customer selects their preferred size and either adds to cart (for online) or locates the item in-store. Analytics capture the entire journey for retailer insights.
