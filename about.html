Reimagining retail.

We're building the future of how people discover and buy fashion.

The problem is massive.

Online fashion has a crisis. 30-40% of clothes bought online get returned because customers can't visualize fit. That's billions in wasted logistics, environmental damage, and frustrated shoppers.

In stores, fitting rooms create bottlenecks. Customers give up after trying on a few items. Retailers lose sales. Everyone loses time.

The technology exists to fix this. But nobody has built it yet.

Our vision.

We're building intelligent mirrors and virtual try-on systems that use computer vision, AI, and real-time 3D physics to let customers see exactly how clothes will look on their body.

No more guessing. No more returns. Just confidence.

For Customers

Try on dozens of outfits in minutes. Compare sizes instantly. See realistic physics simulation. Find your perfect fit without the hassle of changing clothes.

For Retailers

Reduce return rates by 85%. Increase conversions 3x. Gain deep insights into customer preferences. Turn your inventory into an interactive, data-driven experience.

How we're different.

We're not building another photo filter or basic AR overlay. We're creating photorealistic, physics-based simulations that move and drape exactly like real fabric.

Our system captures precise body measurements, matches skin tone for seamless visualization, and renders in real-time at 60fps. The technology stack combines breakthroughs in computer vision, neural networks, and graphics rendering.

What drives us.

  • Customer-First Design Every feature we build starts with the customer experience. Technology should feel magical, not complicated.
  • Retailer Partnership We succeed when retailers succeed. Simple integration, powerful analytics, and real ROI are non-negotiable.
  • Sustainability Reducing returns means less shipping, less waste, and a smaller environmental footprint. Good for business and the planet.
  • Innovation at the Edge We're pushing what's possible with AI and real-time graphics. We invest in R&D that creates lasting competitive advantages.
